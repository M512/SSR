    About SpeedLatch™
    Solar SpeedLatch™ is an innovative universal mounting system designed to reduce the cost, time, and hardware used in the installation of solar systems. Solar SpeedLatch™ is a Self-Grounded system that fits all PV panel sizes with thicknesses between 30mm to 50mm, requires little to no tools or additional parts for installation, and is perfect for all residential and commercial applications. And with its easy release mechanical latch, you can remove panels quickly and easily for repairs, replacements, or in the event of a fire.
